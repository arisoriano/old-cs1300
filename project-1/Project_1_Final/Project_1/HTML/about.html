My name is Ariel and this is my story.

The Somber History

Ever since I can remember I have been obese. Surprisingly enough, no one else in my immediate family is or was. The youngest of three brothers, I was the odd child out growing up prefering to read inside as opposed to joining my superbly athletic brothers outside for a game of basketball or handball (a local NYC sport). I believe my reasoning for not wanting to participate in the physically demanding activities my siblings were partaking of was because of two words: low confidence. The only thing I believed I could muster were the words in my beloved fantasy books and textbooks, nothing else. As you can imagine, such a sedentary livestyle did not complement my body at all and the pounds just kept accumulating. That is when I decided to take action and have my body catch up to what I percieved my true persona to be: healthy and happy. Enough was enough: my obesity was not going to define my identity.

Why I Needed to Lose Weight

Being overweight not only takes a toll on your overall physical health (from joint issues, respiratory problems, disease, you name it) but also your mental state of being. Through most of my teen and young adult life I have endured the psychological torment of my weight, manifested in too many ways to count. There was no quality of life for me, no solace despite having every material thing I needed.

Why I Want To Help

Through my website, I want to educate anyone who is willing to put in 110% of their effort into changing their lifestyles and losing the weight they desire, for health, social, mental, or any reason conceivable. Mainly, though, I want to save people like me before they go through the struggles I did battling my obesity. No one should have to go through that.

How to Use this Site

I learned how to permanently change my unhealthy habits and have successfully lost over 80 lbs -- and kept it off. Because of this, I am confident that I can show and instruct you on how slim down to a more healthy and manageable weight. To begin, you should start at the "Getting Started" link at the top of the page. Don't hesitate in contacting me with any questions via my email on the left side of this page!
